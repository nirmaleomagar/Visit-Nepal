"Lifetime Experiences" in Nepal

Nepal has given the plentiful tourism resources to Nepal. The natural scenery, high mountains, incomparable
cultural heritage and numerous specialties have made Nepal a well known destination in the world of tourism map.
The center for numerous adventure activities such as trekking, mountaineering, rafting, mountain biking, Paragliding,
Jungle Safaris etc. Nepal is widely famous for adventure tourism activities among the visitors from around the world.
Almost a million visitors turn to Nepal to perceive the miraculous mountains each year.
